3.[自动微分](https://github.com/tensorflow/docs/blob/master/site/en/r2/tutorials/eager/automatic_differentiation.ipynb)   
4.[自定义训练：偏差](https://github.com/tensorflow/docs/blob/master/site/en/r2/tutorials/eager/custom_training.ipynb)
